Every site needs a first post, so here it is.

I've been meaning to put a personal site together for a while. Not a portfolio stuffed with screenshots, and not a blog with a backlog of half-finished drafts — just a place to think in public.

## What this will be

Short pieces, mostly. Notes on things I'm building or reading. Occasionally longer essays when something needs more room.

## What this won't be

A content machine. I'd rather write one good thing a month than push out noise to keep an RSS feed alive.

---

That's the plan. We'll see how it goes.
